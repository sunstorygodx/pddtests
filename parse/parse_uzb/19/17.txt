Agar xizmat tormozi to'satdan ishlamay qolsa, mashinani qanday to'xtatish kerak?
Pastroq vitesga o'ting va to'xtash tormozini bosing. Agar kerak bo'lsa, har qanday to'siqdan foydalaning.
Debriyajni o'chiring va to'xtash tormozi bilan keskin tormozlang.
